What TeachToReach is

TeachToReach is a tuition-matching service run by Grace. We connect parents/students looking for a tutor with tutors registered in our pool, and we personally review every match before introducing anyone. We are a matching service, not the employer of any tutor and not a party to any lesson arrangement — the actual tutoring engagement (schedule, fees, lesson terms) is agreed directly between the parent/student and the tutor.

Using the service as a parent

Submitting a request doesn't guarantee a match, and any anonymized instant preview shown on the site is not a confirmed placement — Grace personally confirms and introduces the actual tutor afterwards. You're responsible for agreeing lesson fees, schedule, and conduct directly with the tutor once introduced.

Using the service as a tutor

Registering a profile (via the website or our Telegram bot) doesn't guarantee assignments — Grace reviews and shortlists applicants for each opening. Information you provide should be accurate; misrepresenting your qualifications or experience may result in removal from the pool. You're responsible for your own conduct, punctuality, and professionalism with families you're matched to.

Fees and payment

TeachToReach does not process lesson payments. Any fees for tutoring itself are agreed and paid directly between the parent/student and the tutor. If a placement fee or commission applies, Grace will make that clear to you directly before any introduction is confirmed.
