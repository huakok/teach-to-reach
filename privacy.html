Privacy

Plain language, not legalese — what we collect, why, and what we do with it.

What we collect

When you submit the "Request a tutor" or "Become a tutor" form, we collect what you type in: your name, phone number, email (if given), and your tutoring preferences or profile details — level, subjects, budget, location, availability, and any notes you add. Tutors also give us a Telegram username so future assignment alerts can reach them there.

Why we collect it

Purely to do the matching Grace already does personally — finding the right tutor for the right family. Parent requests are used to find a fitting tutor; tutor profiles are used to shortlist tutors for matching parent requests. That's it — we don't use your details for anything beyond running this service.

Where it's stored

Your information is stored in a secured database (Supabase), accessible only to Grace. The public website itself can only submit new entries — it cannot read back anyone else's data. Nothing you submit is ever sold, and it's not shared with any third party outside of the tutor-matching process itself.

The instant tutor-match preview

When a parent submits a request, the site shows an immediate preview of tutors who might fit — but only anonymized details (tutor tier, subjects, rate range, general area). Names, phone numbers, and Telegram handles are never shown at that stage. Grace personally confirms and introduces the actual tutor afterwards.

How long we keep it

We keep submissions for as long as they're useful for matching — for example, a tutor's profile stays active so they can keep being considered for new requests. If you'd like your information removed at any time, just ask (see contact below) and it'll be deleted.

Questions or removal requests

Reach out to Grace directly — contact details are on the homepage — and she'll sort it personally, same as everything else on this site.
